the problem · the remedy

Without a keeper

  • Results vary run to run.
  • Quality degrades without supervision.
  • “Almost done” stays almost done.
  • Confidence without evidence.

With Babysitter

  • Tasks converge — the loop iterates until the gates pass.
  • Gates are explicit, versioned, re-run each iteration.
  • Done means done: the seal is struck once.
  • Cryptographic proof on every merged PR.
